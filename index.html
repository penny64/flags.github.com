.about

flagsdev LLC. is an independent game developer dedicated to bringing unique concepts to life. Disillusioned with the current trends in indie game development, flagsdev operates under the belief that games should feel good to play, code is an artform, and freeware is still very much alive and well.

.work

Launched along with the revelation of Reactor 3 in 2012, flagsdev existed solely to attach a name to a project. Over a year of development later, Reactor 3 was canceled after its third redesign, and focus was changed to prototyping unique game mechanics. Many prototypes later, flagsdev emerged with the concepts that would later power Insurgence: Shadow Operation, a tactical, squad-based shooter. Featuring procedural generation, formidable AI, and unique visuals, Insurgence proves modern games can still look old-school without being retro.
