What is Reactor 3?

Reactor 3 is a game about survival in the unforgiving landscape surrounding the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant. Featuring a brutal limb-by-limb damage model, day/night cycles, weather events, and challenging NPC behavior, Reactor 3 provides a fresh take on the roguelike genre using both real-time and turn-based gameplay.

Features

  • Intelligent AI that make use of a full range of abilities to reach their goal.
  • Procedurally generated worlds.
  • A realistic damage model complete with full dismemberment, injuries, and item deterioration.
  • Realistic inventory management. Items must be carried, worn, or stored in some kind of container (backpack, pocket, etc.)
  • Full day/night cycle and dynamic lighting.
  • Detailed weapon handling. Weapons must be loaded similar to their real-life counterparts (find ammo, fill magazine/clip, reload.)
  • Make an impact: The results of your actions can be seen in future playthroughs, even after that character is gone.
