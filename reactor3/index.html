Reactor 3

What is Reactor 3?

Reactor 3 is a game about survival. More so, it bridges the gap between the likes of NETHACK, FALLOUT, and S.T.A.L.K.E.R. while introducing roguelike elements. Featuring a procedurally generated world filled with intelligent AI that mangage needs, form relationships, create factions, and start conflicts, Reactor 3 aims to create an immersive experience not previously seen in the roguelike genre.

Features

  • Dynamic AI that explore, loot, form relationships/factions, create conflict, and work together to accomplish tasks.
  • Procedurally generated worlds.
  • A realistic damage model complete with full dismemberment, injuries, and item deterioration.
  • Realistic inventory management. Items must be carried, worn, or stored in some kind of container (backpack, pocket, etc.)
  • Full day/night cycle and dynamic lighting.
  • Vicious wildlife with a range of behaviors, including the simulation of pack mentality.
  • Detailed weapon handling. Weapons must be loaded similar to their real-life counterparts (find ammo, fill magazine/clip, reload.)
  • Make an impact: The results of your actions can be seen in future playthroughs, even after that character is gone.
  • Detailed character backstories.
The following features are still a work in progress.
  • A dialog system for communication between the player and AI.
  • Group job management.
